Tools Of The Trade

What does this bag do?

Nobody said busting into bases would be easy! These otherworldly beanbag artifacts have been popping up across the land with no discernable trace.
Seems like they have something in common with those Light Trees, and word around here says they can be placed down to take out intruders for you!

Spore Bag

Description: A large Puffball mushroom in a loose bag.
Dimensions: 0.7m in diameter.
Grounded: It cushions the connected object from impact forces. It may emit a spore smokescreen if hit hard enough.
In Hand: Allows the player to pound the ground while cushioning fall damage, or deflect attackers with a potent bounce.

Blade Bag

Description: A large sprout with three spiral leaves.
Dimensions: 1m shaft, 1.2m wingspan.
Grounded: It lifts connected objects for a short duration. It can also cut some objects, but expect the shaft to fly off.
In Hand: Allows the player to float/glide for a while, or cut through objects by spinning itself like a bladesaw.

Bam Bag

Description: A large piston-like bamboo shoot.
Dimensions: 1m tall, 30cm diameter disk base.
Grounded: It lifts and pushes pushes objects as it extends and contracts. It can also launch objects that trigger it.
In Hand: Allows the player to shield and respond to attacks with a crushing strike, or aim downwards to launch themself.

Grab Bag

Description: A wound-up vine with six adhesive tips.
Dimensions: 10m vine, 15 cm diameter when coiled.
Grounded: It latches onto objects and pulls them as the vine contracts.
In Hand: Allows the player to swing around structures and grab small objects.
