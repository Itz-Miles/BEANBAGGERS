Tools Of The Trade

Spore Bag

Description: A Puffball mushroom in a loose bag that can cushion falls or pound the ground.
Dimensions: 0.7m in diameter.
Grounded: Allows the player to jump off to cancel downwards momentum or stay on to pound the ground and cushion fall damage.
In Hand: Used to bounce off of or pound the ground. Deforms and emits spores with sufficient impact.

Blade Bag

Description: A spinning sprout with three spiral leaves that provides lift and can cut through objects.
Dimensions: 1.2m wingspan.
Grounded: Provides temporary lift to the player or objects.
In Hand: Spins like a blade saw to cut things. The shaft flies off after 60 seconds of use.

Bam Bag

Description: A rapidly growing bamboo shoot that can lift and crush objects.
Dimensions: 1m tall, 30cm diameter disk base.
Grounded: Lifts and crushes objects as it extends.
In Hand: The bamboo shoot grows instantly and can be used for lifting or crushing when in hand.

Grab Bag

Description: A wound-up vine with adhesive tips that can pull and drag objects as it contracts.
Dimensions: 10m vine, 15 cm diameter when coiled.
Grounded: Latches onto objects and pulls them as the vine contracts.
In Hand: Used to pull and drag objects by latching onto them.
